About Me

I am a software developer with a passion for web development and algorithms and an aptitude for problem-solving and learning quickly. I am a graduate of UC San Diego where I received a global health major and a computer science minor. On April 9th I will be a graduate of App Academy, a 16 week software engineering bootcamp where I became proficient with multiple technologies such as React, Rails, Node, and Express. My strong fundamentals and theoretical knowledge from my computer science minor coupled with my practical skills from App Academy are what give me the ability to be a skilled software developer

On a more personal note, I am an avid raas dancer. Raas is a style of indian dance that is native to the state of Gujarat in India. In college I was a member of UCSD Raas Ruckus, a competitive raas team of which I was captain my senior year. We competed in the national circuit called Raas All-Stars and qualified for the national championship multiple years, placing 4th and 5th.

In my free time I enjoy playing the piano, playing tennis, and playing video games. My favorite composer is Chopin, my favorite tennis player is Nadal, and my favorite video game is either League of Legends or Pokemon
